About

I'm a second year PhD student in the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Washington, advised by James Fogarty and Dan Weld. I received my bachelor's in Computer Science from the University of Michigan, where I worked on crowdsourcing and accessibility with Professor Walter Lasecki. My research spans the intersection of HCI and AI, with a current focus on developing guidelines for error management in human-centered AI applications.

I can be reached at rayfok [at] cs [dot] washington [dot] org.
