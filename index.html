About

I'm a senior at the University of Michigan studying computer science and math. My research interests broadly explore the HCI/AI intersection, with a focus on the design of intelligent systems tying together human intelligence with modern machine learning techniques. My work has studied these hybrid systems with applications in crowdsourcing, communication methods, and accessibility. For the past three years, I have been a member of the Crowds and Machines (CroMa) lab, advised by Walter Lasecki.
